# Experiment Cards

Use these as Copilot or personal build prompts.

## Experiment 001 — Diatom Halo

Build a cyclic symmetric multi-scale Turing renderer with 12-fold symmetry. Add radial rings, pore thresholds, and an opal silica palette.

## Experiment 002 — Fungal Cathedral Bloom

Create radial colony growth with multi-scale Turing interior texture, fuzzy edge, spore dust, and ring pulses.

## Experiment 003 — Art Nouveau Vascular Lace

Extract high-gradient contours from the morphogenesis field, smooth them into whiplash curves, and render as cream/gold/green ornamental linework.

## Experiment 004 — Mesoamerican Cell Glyph Field

Posterize the field into stepped territories. Convert contours to blocky glyph-like bands. Use turquoise, obsidian, maize, bone, and cinnabar.

## Experiment 005 — Crystalline Tissue Armor

Use the morphogenesis field as a heightmap. Overlay Voronoi facets and gradient ridges. Render with opal/quartz structural color.

## Experiment 006 — Embryo Segmentation Weather

Combine a head-tail gradient, repeated segmentation stripes, tissue folding, and Turing noise.

## Experiment 007 — Slime Mold Nerve Map

Run a simple agent trail simulation where agents follow morphogenesis gradients and deposit glowing trail density.

## Experiment 008 — Lichen Territory War

Seed multiple colonies, let them expand, collide, and form scalloped territorial boundaries filled with Turing microtexture.

## Experiment 009 — Shell Memory Scroll

Use polar coordinates where radius acts as developmental time. Generate shell bands, pigment spots, and growth rings.

## Experiment 010 — Wrong Biology Interface

Use morphogenesis fields to generate fake UI panels, buttons, and glyphs as if an organism invented a control surface.
