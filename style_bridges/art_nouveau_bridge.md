# Art Nouveau Bridge

Use morphogenesis fields as plantlike ornamental scaffolds.

## Inputs
- high-gradient contour mask
- thin vein extraction
- cyclic symmetry or bilateral symmetry
- smooth field threshold

## Transform
- extract contour lines
- curl them into whiplash curves
- thicken selected primary veins
- add floral terminals at high-activation nodes
- use low-contrast regions as stained-glass fill

## Visual words
vascular lace, whiplash tendrils, petal-vein morphogens, ornamental tissue, biofiligree, stained-glass cell membrane
